Strategies, communication & structure

Best Strategy

Prepare what you want the interviewer to know, minimize weaknesses, and have questions ready. Research the job and company.

Communication

  • Non-verbal cues & eye contact
  • Active listening
  • Use specifics, examples, and industry terms

6 C's of Effective Communication

Clear, Concise, Correct, Complete, Concrete, Courteous.
