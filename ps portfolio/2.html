Chapter 02 - Portfolio Management

Uses, types, and tips

Uses of a Portfolio

A personal portfolio is a collection of work and achievements that showcases skills and style. Common in creative fields, but useful for many careers.

Types

  • Personal Portfolio
  • Career/Dossier Portfolio
  • Employment Portfolio
  • Assessment Portfolio

Tips

Define purpose, identify audience, organize materials, provide context, update regularly, and back up your work.
