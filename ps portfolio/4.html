Chapter 04 - Crafting an Effective CV

Essential Tips

  • Clear, structured layout with headings and white space
  • Tailor your CV for each role using keywords (helps ATS)
  • Include: Personal info, summary, work experience, education, skills, achievements
  • Avoid spelling errors and irrelevant info

Extras

Add a professional CV image or PDF link and a customizable cover letter.
