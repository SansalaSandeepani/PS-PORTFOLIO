Chapter 11 - Negotiation Skills

BATNA, ZOPA & practical tips

Overview

Prepare thoroughly: set clear goals, know your BATNA (best alternative), identify ZOPA (zone of possible agreement), and build rapport.

Tips

  • Prioritize goals and quantify when possible
  • Use active listening and be willing to walk away
  • Use silence strategically and offer creative solutions
